Impressions of our Negotiation Workshop Part 2

In the second part of our workshop series held together with Business and Finance Club Vienna, we took a game-theoretical approach to negotiation and decision-making, paying special emphasis to its applications to finance! 

Our participants had to roll up their sleeves once more and put their theoretical knowledge to the test in a hands-on, simulated pricing game. At AANCM, we understand that theory can only get us so far; practical application and reflective debriefing based on feedback and discussion are the foundations for long-term learning.
