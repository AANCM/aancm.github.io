Meet Our Team

AANCM has a dedicated team of volunteers who are passionate about promoting the science and the art of negotiation and conflict resolution. 

Our team members bring together a variety of experience, skills, and backgrounds, allowing us to deliver high-quality events, create meaningful opportunities for the community to interact, and provide assistance to individuals and organizations facing difficult circumstances.
