About AANCM

Welcome to the Austrian community of professional negotiators, young professionals, and students dedicated to developing their negotiation skills and learning conflict resolution approaches. Our network is characterized by the diversity of its members, who range from seasoned negotiators to newcomers. We gain our expertise through scientific research, hands-on training, and a multitude of practical experiences that no textbook can provide. Our community offers an environment for individuals to exchange their knowledge, learn new skills, and network with other like-minded people and experts. We are passionate about dispute resolution and negotiation, and we believe in the power of collaboration and introspection to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes. Join us for lectures, discussions, seminars, training sessions, workshops, and conferences on all aspects of negotiation and conflict resolution. Our activities are aimed at providing you with practical ideas, methods, and skills that you can use in your personal and professional lives. Our community welcomes you, whether you are a seasoned negotiator or just starting out. We can learn, grow, and make a difference in the world if we work together. Come and be a part of our dynamic community of negotiators!
