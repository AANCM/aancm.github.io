About AANCM

Who we are

At AANCM, we are committed to supporting individuals and organizations with their negotiation challenges. We are passionate about negotiation and dispute resolution, and we believe in the power of collaboration and introspection to achieve mutually beneficial solutions.

What we offer

We understand that there are many pressing situations in both professional and private contexts that require the ability to communicate effectively and where negotiation is key. Our community offers an environment to exchange knowledge, learn new skills, and network with other like-minded people and experts.

What we believe

AANCM was founded with the belief that everyone can make a difference by learning about negotiation and putting the concepts into practice, no matter what their background is: educators, engineers, lawyers, entrepreneurs, sales, HR, healthcare professionals, students, (project) managers, and business owners - the list is endless.
