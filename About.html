Negotiation and conflict resolution skills play a crucial role in politics, business, and society, as well as in private life. Nevertheless, the relevant knowledge is often only conveyed in specialized seminars and is not considered a standard component in education.

We are an Austrian non-profit organization committed to offering high-quality and trustworthy resources, training opportunities, learning materials, and a networking exchange platform for anyone who is looking to improve their negotiation and conflict resolution skills.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to provide resources to empower interested people to improve their negotiation performance and conflict management skills. We aim to reduce unnecessary conflict, build better relationships, and ultimately create growth opportunities within one's personal sphere through learning and practicing professional negotiation and conflict resolution.

Vision

We believe that life is harder without the ability to negotiate professionally and that by communicating effectively, people can live better together. Our vision is that through our activities, we support people in achieving their goals and improving their personal interactions as well as their careers.
