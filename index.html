Our core activities

Our core activities at AANCM focus on equipping people and communities with effective negotiation skills and dispute resolution strategies, as well as encouraging them to think outside the box when dealing with conflict. Through workshops, seminars, training sessions, and educational materials, we strive to foster professional negotiation and dispute resolution and support people in creating mutually beneficial solutions for achieving their desired outcomes.

Who we are

We are a community of professional negotiators, young professionals, and students committed to developing their negotiation skills and conflict resolution strategies.

What we offer

Our community offers an environment to exchange knowledge, learn new skills, and network with other like-minded people and experts while having a fun experience.

What we believe

Our organization was founded with the belief that everyone can make a difference by learning about negotiation and conflict resolution - no matter what their background is.
